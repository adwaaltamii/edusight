Get to Know Us



Education is an essential aspect of life as choosing your place of education directs and paves the way for your life, career, and future. It is crucial to obtain information about educational institutions, such as the type, curriculum, and required education individuals and parents need. The problem of finding suitable educational institutions such as schools, universities, and institutes affects individuals and parents when searching for their children's institutions, the impact of which is difficulty finding qualified institutions in one place and problem filtering among them to make the right decision and choose the most suitable institution.

A successful website would be EduSight, a simple online platform that combines educational institutions, including several details and providing a vast number of private tutors. The product would be beneficial for users to simplify the searching process providing easy filtration options, therefore, shortening the search time.
